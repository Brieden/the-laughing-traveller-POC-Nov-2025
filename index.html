Who is this Guy?

Hola! I'm Fernando. Your favorite professional overthinker.

The "Aha" Moment

I moved from Costa Rica to Germany and had a rough start. I was frustrated, angry, and crying in my apartment watching Gabriel "Fluffy" Iglesias. I thought, "If this guy can make me laugh while I'm miserable, maybe I can do that for others."

Then I went to a comedy show in Frankfurt. I saw a guy bombing terribly with jokes about glory holes. I sat there and thought: "I can definitely do better than that." And just like that, a comedian was born.

My Style
